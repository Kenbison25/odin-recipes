Simple Protein Overnight Oats!

Description

Sometimes in the never-ending hustle and bustle, you need a snack or a breakfast that is quick, easy, and is also super convenient on the wallet and on your time. I present to you... the Overnight Oats!

[Image Will Be Placed Here Once I Make it Again!]

Like the meal, let's keep this short and sweet...

    INGREDIENTS:
  • Oats of any kind (but rolled oats is best)
  • Milk of any kind (I use Fairlife fat-free for BEST GAINZ)
  • Bananas, or other fruit (Optional)
  • Protein Powder (Optional - but good FOR GAINZZ)

HERE ARE THE INSTRUCTIONS!

  1. Put the oats and milk and protein powder into a sealable container.
  2. Let it sit overnight!
  3. Grab it int he morning (or hours after you made it) and add fruit if ya want!
